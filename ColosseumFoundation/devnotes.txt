Regarding Damage Modifier Priority:

-3 is for very slow blows: takes little skill to avoid
-2 is for normal strikes: takes some skill to avoid
-1 is for slow projectiles and quick strikes: can be avoided with skill
0 is for fast projectiles and lightning-fast strikes: normally unavoidable
1 is for blinking fast projectiles: incredibly unavoidable
2 is for linked attacks: requires substantial unhuman skill to avoid
3 is for modifiers of all attacks
